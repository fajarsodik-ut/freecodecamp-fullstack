YOASOBI

YOASOBI is a Japanese pop duo formed in 2019, consisting of composer Ayase and vocalist ikura. They are known for creating songs based on novels and short stories, blending electronic music with pop melodies. Their breakthrough hit "Yoru ni Kakeru" became a viral sensation and established them as one of Japan's most popular musical acts.

Probably

"Probably" is one of YOASOBI's popular tracks that showcases their signature style of combining emotional storytelling with upbeat electronic pop melodies.
